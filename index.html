The Reading Crisis & Our Mission

Did you know that 65% of children never develop a love for reading?

While screens dominate our children's attention, the magic of books is slowly fading away. But here's the thing – reading isn't just about entertainment. It's about building the future leaders, thinkers, and dreamers of tomorrow.

The Silent Crisis in Our Communities

Why Reading Changes Everything

🧠

Cognitive Development

Reading increases vocabulary by 50% and improves critical thinking skills. Children who read regularly score 20% higher on standardized tests.

💭

Empathy & Understanding

Books transport us into different worlds, cultures, and perspectives. Readers develop 30% higher emotional intelligence and cultural awareness.

🎯

Future Success

Reading for just 20 minutes daily puts children in the 90th percentile of academic performance. It's the single most important predictor of success.

🔗

Social Connection

Shared stories create bonds that last a lifetime. Reading together builds stronger family relationships and community connections.

The Barriers We Face

The digital age has created an unintended consequence: While information is more accessible than ever, deep reading has become a lost art. Children are bombarded with quick, fragmented content that doesn't require sustained attention or critical thinking.

Many parents struggle to find the right books, don't know how to make reading engaging, or simply lack the time to guide their children's literary journey. Schools focus on standardized testing rather than fostering genuine love for literature.

The result? A generation that can consume information rapidly but struggles with deep comprehension, empathy, and the kind of sustained focus that builds character and wisdom.

73% of parents feel overwhelmed choosing appropriate books
89% of children prefer digital entertainment over reading
56% of families read together less than once per week
